FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?
We ship orders within 24-48 hours. Domestic orders typically arrive in 3-5 business days. International shipping takes 7-14 business days depending on location. You'll receive tracking information via email once your order ships.
What's your return policy?
We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, simply contact us for a return label. Items must be in original condition with all packaging. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your return.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Customs fees and import duties are the responsibility of the buyer and are not included in our shipping charges.
Are your products authentic?
Absolutely. We source all products directly from manufacturers or authorized distributors. Every item comes with full manufacturer warranty. We never sell counterfeit or refurbished items as new.
How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can use this number on our carrier's website to track your package in real-time. If you haven't received tracking within 48 hours of ordering, please contact support.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption for your protection.
